Executive Summary

EduTrack AI is an AI-powered learning companion designed to help Indian college students identify skill gaps, receive personalized study roadmaps, and track their placement readiness in real-time. The platform targets the underserved tier-2 and tier-3 city student population, offering an affordable subscription (₹99–299/mo) with measurable placement outcomes.

The product addresses a critical pain point: students know they need to upskill but lack clarity on what to learn, in what order, and whether they're progressing. EduTrack AI solves this with job-market-linked AI that translates ambition into a structured, trackable action plan.

Target Launch: Q3 2025 · Initial Market: India Tier-2 cities · Revenue Model: B2C subscription + B2B institutional licensing

Product Objectives

  • Achieve 10,000 active users within 6 months of launch
  • Maintain 30-day retention above 45% (vs industry avg 32%)
  • Generate ₹1Cr ARR by month 12
  • Onboard 20 colleges on the institutional dashboard by Q4 2025
  • Measure and report measurable skill improvement for 80% of active users

Out of Scope (v1)

  • Live instructor-led classes
  • Job application management
  • Employer-facing hiring tools

Core Features — v1

  • AI Skill Gap Analyzer: NLP-based analysis of student profile vs top 50 job descriptions in target role
  • Personalized Study Path: Week-by-week plan generated from gap score, available time, and deadline
  • Progress Dashboard: Radar chart of competency areas, completion tracking, streak counter
  • Job Role Matching: Top 5 matched roles with fit % and gap breakdown per role
  • Resource Curator: Curated free + paid resources for each skill, ranked by quality and student ratings
  • Mobile PWA: Offline-capable progressive web app for Android and iOS

User Stories

US-001
As a student, I want to see which skills I'm missing for my target role, so I know exactly what to learn first.
AC: Given a student profile and target role, when analysis runs, then display top 5 skill gaps ranked by impact with resource links.
US-002
As a student, I want a weekly study plan tailored to my schedule, so I can learn consistently without overwhelm.
AC: Given availability and deadline, generate a day-by-day plan with estimated completion time per task.
US-003
As a placement officer, I want to see which students are at risk of not being placement-ready, so I can intervene early.
AC: Dashboard shows red/amber/green status for all enrolled students with drill-down to individual skill reports.
US-004
As a student, I want to see my progress over time as a visual chart, so I feel motivated by my improvement.
AC: Show weekly competency radar chart that evolves as students complete modules. Show % growth vs onboarding baseline.

Success Metrics

  • Activation: % of signups who complete skill assessment within 24hrs → Target: 60%
  • Engagement: Daily Active Users / Monthly Active Users → Target: 0.35
  • Retention: 30-day retention → Target: 45%
  • Revenue: MRR at month 6 → Target: ₹8L
  • Placement Impact: % of active users reporting improved placement outcomes → Target: 70%
  • NPS: Net Promoter Score → Target: 45+

02 — Market Research

The India EdTech market is projected to reach $8.2B by 2025. AI personalization tools are growing at 47% CAGR. Key opportunities exist in tier-2 city penetration, placement-linked learning, and institutional B2B analytics — all underserved by current players.
